Farnborough Palace

The Farnborough Palace adjoins the grade one listed 13th Century Farnborough tower. This historic building is situated in the walled gardens, ancient woodland and private surrounding land of Farnborough estate.
This beautiful building offers a gracious taste of luxury country living with elegance and comfort in abundance.
The spacious Farnborough room has luxury sofas and a feature fire place with log burner for those colder nights. There is a fully fitted kitchen with all the necessary amenities and ground floor bathroom and toilet.
The opulence continues with the 5 luxury en-suite bedrooms, rainfall showers and classically styled baths. Three of which can be made into twin rooms. One of the en suite bedrooms is situated on the ground floor for convenience, with beautiful garden views.
The Farnborough opens out to an impressive suntrap courtyard which offers a stylish area for a BBQ, drinks, private dining and relaxation.
With a variety of areas to stroll or to sit and relax in the garden and parkland, it is a wonderful space to explore.
The Ancient tree lined drive is a kilometre long so there is great privacy and uninterrupted views.
